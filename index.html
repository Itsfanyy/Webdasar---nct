NCT Dream

NCT Dream is a South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment in 2016. The group consists of six members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, and Chenle. Originally intended to be a rotational unit, NCT Dream became a fixed unit in 2017 with the graduation of Mark.

NCT Dream's music is known for its bright and upbeat sound, with a focus on themes of youth and positivity. Their debut single "Chewing Gum" was a catchy pop song that showcased the group's youthful energy and charm. Since then, they have released several successful singles and albums, including "My First and Last," "We Young," and "Reload."

In addition to their music, NCT Dream is also known for their energetic and dynamic performances. They often incorporate intricate choreography and acrobatics into their live shows, showcasing their impressive dancing skills and athleticism.

Overall, NCT Dream has become one of the most popular and beloved boy groups in the K-pop industry, with a dedicated fanbase around the world. Their music and performances continue to inspire and uplift fans, making them a true force to be reckoned with in the industry.
